Acted as the Technical Executive for the START 2013 Conference.

During the 2012 - 2013 school year, I was the technical executive for the conference. As such, I was responsible for handling promotional affairs prior to the conference date as well as audiovisual affairs on the conference day. During the year, I designed promotional posters, logos and the website for the conference using Photoshop and Dreamweaver.
